Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

Last Updated: July 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

MacroMate collects the following types of information to provide you with the best possible experience:

  • Location Data: We use your location to find nearby restaurants and provide personalized recommendations. Location access is only used when you explicitly request restaurant discovery features.
  • Usage Information: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app's performance and user experience.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and preferences.
  • Menu Scan Data: When you scan menus, we process the images locally on your device for analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Restaurant Discovery: Your location is used solely to show nearby restaurants and relevant menu hacks.
  • Personalization: We use your preferences to provide customized menu recommendations.
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us enhance features and fix bugs.
  • Support: We may use your contact information to respond to support requests.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • In connection with a business transfer (with notice to you)

4. Location Data

MacroMate uses location services to:

  • Find restaurants near you
  • Provide distance-based recommendations
  • Show relevant local menu options

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, though this may limit some app functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

MacroMate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find restaurants near me?

MacroMate uses your device's location to show nearby restaurants. Make sure location services are enabled for the app in your device settings.

Why do I need to enable location access?

Location access allows MacroMate to show you restaurants in your area and provide relevant menu recommendations. Without location access, you can still browse restaurants and menu hacks, but you won't see distance-based recommendations.

How do menu scans work?

Menu scanning uses AI to analyze restaurant menus and provide macro-friendly recommendations. This feature is currently in development and will be available in a future update.

Can I use MacroMate without an internet connection?

MacroMate requires an internet connection to load restaurant data and menu information. However, your favorites and recent searches are cached locally.

How do I report a problem or suggest a feature?

You can contact us through the app's feedback feature or email us directly at support@macromate.app.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues, try these steps:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of MacroMate
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Restart the app
  4. Restart your device
  5. If problems persist, contact us at support@macromate.app

About MacroMate
